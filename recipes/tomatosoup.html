Tomato Soup

My own version of tomato soup if you can call it that.
Every version of tomato soup is likely the same, this one comes with an asian twist.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 liters of water
  • 1 kg of ripe tomatoes
  • 1 can (around 400 grams) of peeled tomatoes
  • 1 can (140 gram) of tomato paste
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbps ginger syrup
  • 3 vegetable stock cubes
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Peel and cut the onion and cloves of garlic, add olive oil to a pan and cook until softened.
  2. Add the tomato paste and saut√© until some caramelization occurs.
  3. Cut the tomatoes into quarters, add them and the peeled tomatoes to the pan, cook until the skin releases from the tomatoes.
  4. Add water, ginger syrup and the vegetable stock cubes to the pan, bring to a boil.
  5. Take the pan off the heat and blitz everything together until smooth, add pepper to taste.
